NSBM Green University Town Sports has something for every student ensuring that the students have the best sport experience in Sri Lanka. A sophisticated recreation center is open for all students with its modern gymnasium, swimming pool, sports ground and many other facilities. Several Sports clubs have been initiated inside the University to offer unique and exciting opportunities for students to participate in competitions and also to stay physically fit as an undergraduate.
