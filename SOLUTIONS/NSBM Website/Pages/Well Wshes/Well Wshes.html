       Well Wishes

Paving way to making thousands of students’ dreams a reality, the NSBM Green University Town was ceremoniously inaugurated and bestowed upon the nation on the 26th of October, 2016. The NSBM Green University Town is the first and only one of its’ kind in the whole of South Asia dedicated to excellence in sustainable education.

We are proud to say that our schools of Business, Computing and Engineering are of the highest standard both technically and educationally. The Degree programs offered at NSBM are carefully designed to suit the requirements of the modern, constantly evolving business world. Hence, we have invested in the latest facilities in order to provide our undergraduates with new learning experiences at every stage of their academic years at NSBM. Thus, for effective implementation NSBM recruits only the best professionals with strong academic as well as industrial backgrounds, with the undeniable ability to guide our students both theoretically and practically. To add more value to our education, NSBM collaborates with prestigious, world renowned universities such as, University College Dublin, Ireland, The University of Plymouth, UK and Victoria University, Australia – providing undergraduates with internationally recognized degrees.

So we hope you will make the wise decision of choosing NSBM for your higher education. Join us and step into the future of world class education.
